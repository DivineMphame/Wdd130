South Africa

South Africa, located at the southern tip of Africa, is known for its diverse landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and complex history. It is bordered by Namibia, Botswana, Zimbabwe, Mozambique, Swaziland, and Lesotho, the latter of which is an enclave within South African territory. The country boasts a coastline along both the Atlantic and Indian Oceans.
